More information about the project

Always detail some important features.

Linear Movement

To move around in the Maze, you can use the 'w','a','s','d' keys on your keyboard, to move forward, left, backwards, and right accordingly.

Rotation

For simplicity, you can rotate your camera using your touchpad. This way, you can face any direction you want.

Exit

Finally to exit, simply press "Alt+f4". this provides a simple exit out of the game.

Have fun and hope you enjoy this simple and easy to use 3d Maze
